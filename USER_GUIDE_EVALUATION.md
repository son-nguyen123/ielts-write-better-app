# User Experience Guide: Enhanced IELTS Evaluation

## What's New

The IELTS essay evaluation system now provides a **complete revised version** of your essay along with detailed feedback.

## How It Works

### 1. Submit Your Essay
- Write or paste your IELTS Task 1 or Task 2 essay
- Select the appropriate task type and prompt
- Choose your preferred Gemini model (optional)
- Click "Submit for Evaluation"

### 2. View Comprehensive Feedback

#### Overall Score Card
- **Band Score**: Your overall IELTS band (0-9 with 0.5 increments)
- **Summary**: Brief assessment of your essay

#### Criterion-by-Criterion Analysis
For each of the four IELTS criteria (TR, CC, LR, GRA):
- **Score**: Individual band score for this criterion
- **Strengths**: What you did well
- **Areas for Improvement**: Where you can improve
- **Suggestions**: Specific actionable advice
- **Examples**: References to your actual writing

#### Line-Level Feedback (Sentence-level Feedback Tab)
- **Highlighted Errors**: Each error is highlighted in your essay
- **Error Categories**: Grammar, Lexical, Coherence, or Task Response
- **Explanations**: Clear explanation of each issue
- **Corrections**: Suggested rewrites for problematic sections

### 3. Access Your Revised Essay (NEW!)

#### Suggested Improvements Tab
- **Complete Revised Essay**: A fully corrected and improved version
- **All Corrections Applied**: Grammar, vocabulary, coherence enhanced
- **Original Ideas Preserved**: Your arguments and ideas remain intact
- **Model Answer**: Use it as a reference for future writing

#### Key Improvements Section
- **Action Items**: Prioritized list of improvements to focus on
- **Learning Points**: Key takeaways from the evaluation

## Tips for Using the Revised Essay

### Learning Tool
- Compare your original with the revised version
- Identify patterns in your errors
- Learn new vocabulary and structures

### Practice Reference
- Use as a model for similar topics
- Note the paragraph structure and flow
- Observe how ideas are developed

### Progress Tracking
- Save both versions for comparison
- Track improvement across multiple revisions
- Set goals based on recurring issues

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: Is the revised essay generated by AI?
**A:** Yes, it's generated by Gemini AI based on your original essay. It maintains your ideas while improving the expression.

### Q: Can I see what changed?
**A:** The line-level feedback shows specific corrections. For a full comparison, use the "Compare Versions" feature.

### Q: What model is used?
**A:** The default model is gemini-2.0-flash, but you can select different Gemini models when re-evaluating.

### Q: How do I improve my score?
**A:** Focus on the "Key Improvements" section and review the line-level feedback. The revised essay shows how to implement these improvements.

### Q: Can I re-evaluate after making changes?
**A:** Yes! Click "Edit & Re-evaluate" to modify your essay and get new feedback. All revisions are saved in the timeline.
